# Support Knowledge Base

---

# 1. General Issues

### Q: How do I create an account?

1. Click the **Sign Up** button.
2. Enter your name, email, and password.
3. Verify your email address.
4. Log in to access your account.

---

### Q: I forgot my password.

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Follow the instructions in the reset email.

If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder.

---

### Q: How can I update my profile information?

1. Log in to your account.
2. Open **Profile Settings**.
3. Update the required information.
4. Save the changes.

---

### Q: How can I contact support?

Please provide:

* Your registered email address
* A detailed description of the issue
* Relevant screenshots if available

Our support team will assist you as soon as possible.

---

# 2. Technical Issues

### Q: The website is not loading.

Try the following:

1. Refresh the page.
2. Clear your browser cache.
3. Check your internet connection.
4. Try another browser or device.

---

### Q: I am getting an error message.

Please provide:

* Screenshot of the error
* Browser name and version
* Steps to reproduce the issue

This information helps us investigate faster.

---

### Q: Features are not working correctly.

Possible solutions:

* Log out and log back in.
* Clear browser cache and cookies.
* Disable browser extensions.
* Try another browser.

If the issue persists, contact support.

---

### Q: The application is running slowly.

Try:

* Refreshing the page
* Closing unnecessary browser tabs
* Using a stable internet connection
* Clearing browser cache

---

# 3. Refund Issues

### Q: How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, provide:

* Registered email address
* Order ID
* Reason for the refund request

Our team will review your request according to the refund policy.

---

### Q: How long does the refund process take?

Refund requests are typically reviewed within 3–7 business days.

Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider.

---

### Q: My refund has been approved but I have not received the money.

Refund processing depends on your payment provider and bank.

Please allow additional processing time before contacting support.

---

### Q: Am I eligible for a refund?

Eligibility depends on:

* Purchase date
* Product or service purchased
* Compliance with the refund policy

Our support team will verify eligibility after reviewing your request.
